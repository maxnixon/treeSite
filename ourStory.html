About Atreeum Tree Service

Atreeum Tree Services is a single father-owned business, that has been serving the tri-state area since the beginning of 2017. There are many different aspects of caring for and servicing trees. Whether it is a simple pruning and trimming, or diagnosing various problems you may encounter with your trees. Atreeum Tree Services is the simple solution.

The staff at Atreeum Tree Service is educated, trained and have Certiﬁed Arborists, on staff. Atreeum is readily available, responsive and understanding to all of our customers’ needs.

We specialize in everything from tree pruning to tree removal, emergency work and disease control. We strive to provide our clients with personalized one on one service. We understand that mother nature has a way of sneaking up on you, out of nowhere.

Meet Joeseph Kemerle/Valentine ~ Founder/Owner Atreeum Tree Service "The Sky Is The Limit

Not much long later Joseph experienced some hardships and had to move back to NJ with nothing but his vehicle, climbing gear, and his chain saw. Before "Atreeum Tree Service" became an official company Joesph started soliciting to use the skills he knew to provide for his daughter.
